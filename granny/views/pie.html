Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6 tbsp white sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat Oven: 350°F (175°C).
  2. Make Lemon Filling: Mix sugar, flour, cornstarch, and salt...
  3. Make Meringue: Whip egg whites...
  4. Bake: Bake for 10 minutes until golden brown.
