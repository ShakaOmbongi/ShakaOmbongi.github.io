Grandma's hidden Secret Lemon Meringue Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6 tbsp white sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a medium saucepan, whisk together white sugar, flour, cornstarch, and salt. Stir in water, lemon juice, and lemon zest. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium-high heat, stirring constantly, until thickened. Remove from heat and quickly stir in butter and egg yolks.
  3. Pour the lemon filling into the baked pie crust.
  4. In a separate bowl, beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form. Gradually add the remaining white sugar and continue beating until the meringue is glossy. Spread the meringue over the hot filling, sealing the edges at the crust.
  5. Bake for 10 minutes, or until the meringue is golden brown.
  6. Allow the pie to cool before serving.

Granny's Secret Recipe Tips

Secret Ingredient: Add a pinch of ground cardamom to the lemon filling. This little twist gives the pie an extra burst of flavor that’s been Granny’s secret for generations!

Tip: Let the lemon filling cool slightly before adding the meringue to prevent it from deflating, ensuring a light and fluffy topping.

Fun Fact
